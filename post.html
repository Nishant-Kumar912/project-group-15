Comparison of new income tax regime with old tax regime

Mar 1, 2020

The Union Budget 2020 introduced a new personal tax regime for individual taxpayers. However, the option for such concessional tax regime requires the taxpayer to forego certain specified deductions. These include standard deduction of Rs 50,000, deduction under section 80C of Rs 1.50 lakh and the interest on self-occupied property of Rs 2 lakh which are availed by most taxpayers. As a result, the concessional tax regime may not always be beneficial.
